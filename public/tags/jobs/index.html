Who Am I? Job Hunting Edition

Jarrett Byrnes
Oct 23, 2008

As I am in the thick of job hunting season, I’m writing a few applications. One of the most interesting parts of the whole process is the research statement. It’s interesting as it forces one to really be self-reflective, and ask “Who am I and why am I doing this?” The first paragraph in particular is a sort of declaration. Hopefully this will be the last one of these I write, but, who am I kidding.
