A Preprint Experiment: Four Pillars and a Foundation for the Future of Scholarly Publishing

Jarrett Byrnes
May 7, 2013

x-post from the OpenPub Project blog So, we got together, had two working group meetings to discuss the future of scholarly publishing in Ecology, Evolutionary Biology, and the Earth and Ocean Sciences. What were were thinking that entire time? We’ve just submitted a piece that brings together our broad ideas (some of which have been seen before), but, simultaneous to publication, we’ve also decided to put up a preprint. Why? Simply put, immediate access is one of our four pillars of the future of scholarly publication.
