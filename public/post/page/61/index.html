Going Topless with Urchins

Jarrett Byrnes
Jun 1, 2009

There’s nothing so satisfying as pulling back and seeing your brand new experiment out there in the water. It’s been a crazy week or three getting this up and running, but now my first big postdoctoral experiment is soaking in the water, with urchins grazing away. I’m testing some ideas regarding how diversity mediates the impact of disturbance by urchin grazing, and vice-versa - how disturbance by grazing can alter diversity.
