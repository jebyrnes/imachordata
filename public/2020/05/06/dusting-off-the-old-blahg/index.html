Dusting off the old blahg

Jarrett Byrnes
May 6, 2020

Welp. It’s been a spell. Time to bring ye olde blahg back.

As I moved talking about marine science more on to Deep Sea News, I realized that there’s a huge part of my identity as a scientist that didn’t work there.

Data nerdery.

As many of those posts were written in RMarkdown, I took the plunge and have remade this into a blogdown site. It’s my first venture here. I’m just glad I managed to port all of my old posts over!

There’s definitely some things still to do - a comments section, etc - but, in the meantime, if you find some dust around the edges or a poor transition, now you can file an issue on this blog’s github repo! HA!

But, we shall see how rebooting this goes. I’ve got a few things to write about.
